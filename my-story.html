My Story

My career path and journey to the field of UX and tech more broadly is somewhat different than most. I studied Anthropology, History, and modern languages in college, and I initially wanted to be a teacher and professor. I completed a PhD in History and wrote a dissertation on the impacts of US militarism on women in the Philippines.

I really enjoyed academic research and teaching, but I felt like something was missing for me. There was a lot of focus on understanding social problems but not as much on designing solutions. This is what eventually led me to pursue UX design and web development.
